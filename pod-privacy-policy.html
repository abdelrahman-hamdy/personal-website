Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2025

Welcome to People Of Data, the mobile app designed for the AI community in Egypt and, soon, around the world. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our application.

What Information We Collect

We collect only essential information required to provide a smooth and personalized user experience. This includes:

  • Basic User Information: Name, email, and other profile data.
  • Experience History & Portfolio: Information related to your career, freelance work, or past project involvement.

We do not collect any sensitive personal data such as government IDs, financial details, or biometric information.

Permissions and Device Access

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for basic app functionality such as logging in, viewing events, chatting, and applying for jobs.
  • Gallery Access: Only used to allow you to upload an optional profile picture. This access is not required to use the app.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your profile.
  • Enable participation in events and hackathons.
  • Allow communication between users via chat.
  • Apply for or post freelance jobs.
  • Improve the app’s features and user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your information is only used within the app and stored securely.

Security

We take data protection seriously. Industry-standard encryption and security measures are used to ensure your data remains safe and private.

Your Rights

You can view, edit, or delete your profile data at any time through the app settings.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services. When we do, we’ll notify users through the app or update the effective date above.
